<p><ul><li>Based on Inertial System</li><li>Based on Optical System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Biomechanics Tracking System market is primarily segmented into two types: Inertial Systems and Optical Systems. Inertial Systems utilize accelerometers and gyroscopes to measure motion and position changes in real time, offering a portable and flexible solution for biomechanics analysis. Optical Systems, on the other hand, deploy cameras and markers to capture precise movements through image processing, providing high accuracy in motion capture but often requiring a controlled environment. Each system caters to different research and clinical applications in biomechanics.</p></p>
