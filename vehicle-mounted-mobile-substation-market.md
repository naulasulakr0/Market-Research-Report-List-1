<p><strong>The Vehicle-mounted Mobile Substation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HV Mobile Substation</li><li>LV and MV Mobile Substation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle-mounted mobile substation market includes various types based on voltage levels. High Voltage (HV) mobile substations are designed to handle transmission-level voltages, offering rapid deployment for emergency situations and maintenance. Low Voltage (LV) mobile substations cater to distribution needs, suitable for urban areas and small-scale applications. Medium Voltage (MV) mobile substations bridge the gap between LV and HV, providing flexibility in voltage management for temporary setups in industrial or commercial environments, ensuring reliable power supply during outages or expansions.</p></p>
