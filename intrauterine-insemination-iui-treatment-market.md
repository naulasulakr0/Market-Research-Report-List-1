<p><p>The Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) treatment market encompasses various methods of sperm delivery into the female reproductive system. Intrauterine insemination directly places sperm into the uterus, maximizing chances of fertilization. Intracervical insemination introduces sperm into the cervix, a less invasive approach. Intravaginal insemination involves placing sperm in the vaginal canal, relying on natural sperm migration. Intratubal insemination delivers sperm directly into the fallopian tubes, targeting fertilization close to the egg, each method catering to specific fertility needs and circumstances.</p></p>
