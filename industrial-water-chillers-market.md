<p><ul><li>Screw Chillers</li><li>Centrifugal Chillers</li><li>Reciprocating Chillers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial water chillers market comprises various types tailored for specific cooling needs. Screw chillers utilize twin-screw compressors for efficient, continuous operation, making them suitable for large applications. Centrifugal chillers leverage centrifugal force for high-capacity cooling, ideal for commercial use. Reciprocating chillers employ piston-driven compressors, offering flexibility for smaller loads. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialized chillers, such as absorption and scroll chillers, catering to niche applications, enhancing versatility in industrial cooling solutions.</p></p>
