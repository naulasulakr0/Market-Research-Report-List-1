<p><strong>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting Magnet</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI equipment market can be categorized mainly into two types of magnets: superconducting and permanent magnets. Superconducting magnets are widely used due to their ability to produce strong magnetic fields, allowing for high-resolution imaging. These magnets require cooling to maintain superconductivity. In contrast, permanent magnets generate a stable magnetic field without the need for cooling, making them more energy-efficient and portable, though typically offering lower imaging quality. Each type serves specific clinical needs and applications in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
