<p><p>Cold In-place Recycling (CIR) machines are specialized equipment used in the road construction and maintenance industry. They allow for the rehabilitation of existing asphalt pavements by milling and reusing the material, reducing the need for new materials and minimizing environmental impact. CIR processes involve the removal of the top layers of the pavement, which are then crushed, screened, and mixed with new binder materials to create a high-quality, durable surface.</p><p>The Cold In-place Recycling (CIR) Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for sustainable construction practices and effective road maintenance solutions.
