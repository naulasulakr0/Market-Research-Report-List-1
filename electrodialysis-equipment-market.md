Continuous Electrodialysis operates in a steady flow, allowing for constant separation of ions in processes like desalination and wastewater treatment, making it efficient for large-scale applications. In contrast, Batch Electrodialysis processes involve treating fixed amounts of liquid at a time, ideal for smaller operations or where flexibility is needed. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing ion separation and purification technologies.</p></p>
