<p><p>The EVA resin market can be categorized into three main types: Tubular EVA, Autoclave EVA, and Other Processes. Tubular EVA is produced using a tubular reactor, offering high efficiency and consistent quality, making it suitable for various applications like films and foams. Autoclave EVA, manufactured in a pressurized environment, provides enhanced properties and is often used in adhesives and coatings. Other Processes encompass alternative manufacturing methods, allowing for specialized formulations and unique applications in diverse industries, such as automotive and packaging.</p></p>
