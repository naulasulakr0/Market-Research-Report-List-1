<p><p>The differential pressure flow meter market includes various types that measure fluid flow based on pressure differences. Orifice flow meters feature a plate with an opening that creates a pressure drop. Venturi flow meters utilize a tapered tube to measure flow with minimal pressure loss. Wedge flow meters use a wedge-shaped obstruction to generate differential pressure, while laminar flow meters measure flow at low velocities through a straight, narrow passage. Other types may include flow meters based on ultrasonic or magnetic principles, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
