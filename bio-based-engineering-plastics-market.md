<p><p>The bio-based engineering plastics market encompasses various processing techniques, primarily injection molding and extrusion. Injection molding involves injecting melted bio-based plastics into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and high production efficiency, making it ideal for complex parts. In contrast, extrusion involves forcing melted bio-based plastics through a die to create continuous profiles like sheets or pipes, suitable for various applications. Both methods leverage renewable materials to produce sustainable, high-performance products for industries like automotive, packaging, and consumer goods.</p></p>
