<p><p>The liquid formulations drug delivery market includes various types, primarily suspensions and solutions. Suspensions consist of solid drug particles dispersed in a liquid, providing extended release and improved bioavailability for poorly soluble drugs. Solutions, on the other hand, are homogenous mixtures with dissolved drugs, offering immediate action and consistent dosing. Both formulations enhance patient compliance and facilitate administration, catering to various therapeutic needs and improving treatment outcomes in diverse patient populations.</p></p>
