<p><p>The safety valve market primarily includes control valves and diaphragm valves, both essential for pressure regulation and safety in various industrial applications. Control valves manage fluid flow by adjusting their opening based on specific conditions, ensuring system stability. Diaphragm valves, on the other hand, utilize a flexible membrane to control flow, providing excellent sealing and isolation. Both types are critical in preventing hazardous conditions, regulating pressure, and ensuring operational safety in sectors like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
