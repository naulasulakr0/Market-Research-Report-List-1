<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Absorbers (UVA)</li><li>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet Stabilizer market is segmented into three main types: UV Absorbers (UVA), Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), and others. UV Absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation and convert it into harmless heat, protecting materials from degradation. HALS, on the other hand, work by scavenging free radicals generated during UV exposure, enhancing durability and resistance. Other stabilizers may include inorganic UV stabilizers or hybrid systems, which provide additional protective effects, catering to diverse applications in plastics, coatings, and textiles.</p></p>
