<p><p>The artificial inorganic membranes market features primarily two types: flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes are typically used in filtration processes, offering a large surface area for separation and purification in various industries, including water treatment and chemical processing. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, are designed for continuous flow applications, optimizing fluid transport and separation in pipelines. Both types leverage inorganic materials for enhanced durability, thermal stability, and resistance to chemical degradation, making them suitable for challenging environments.</p></p>
