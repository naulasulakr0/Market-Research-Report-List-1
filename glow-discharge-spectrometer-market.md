<p><p>The Glow Discharge Spectrometer (GDMS) market comprises two main types: DC-GDMS and RF-GDMS. DC-GDMS operates using direct current to generate plasma, allowing for the analysis of conductive samples, particularly metals. In contrast, RF-GDMS utilizes radio frequency energy to produce plasma, making it suitable for a broader range of materials, including insulators and non-metals. Both techniques offer high sensitivity and precision, serving industries such as metallurgy, semiconductor manufacturing, and materials science for elemental analysis.</p></p>
