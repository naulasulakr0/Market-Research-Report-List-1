<p><ul><li>Line Pumps</li><li>Boom Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction concrete pump market comprises primarily line pumps and boom pumps, each serving distinct applications. Line pumps are ideal for smaller volume projects, allowing the transport of concrete through hoses over considerable distances, making them suitable for residential and interior work. In contrast, boom pumps are equipped with a mechanical arm, providing greater reach and efficiency for large-scale projects like high-rise buildings and extensive infrastructure. Both types improve productivity, speed, and precision in concrete placement across various construction sites.</p></p>
