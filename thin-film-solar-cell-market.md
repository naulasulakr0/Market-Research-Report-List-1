<p><p>The thin film solar cell market includes three main types: CdTe, CIGS, and GaAs. CdTe (Cadmium Telluride) cells are cost-effective and efficient for large-scale production, making them popular in utility-scale applications. CIGS (Copper Indium Gallium Selenide) offers flexibility and higher efficiencies, suitable for building-integrated photovoltaics. GaAs (Gallium Arsenide) cells are known for their high efficiency and performance in space applications, though they are more expensive to produce. Together, these technologies contribute to diverse solar energy solutions.</p></p>
