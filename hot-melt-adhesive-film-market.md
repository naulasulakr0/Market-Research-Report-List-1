<p><p>Hot Melt Adhesive Film (HMAF) is a thermoplastic polymer-based adhesive used for bonding materials without the need for curing agents or solvents. The versatility and ease of application of HMAF contribute to their increasing demand across various sectors.</p><p>The Hot Melt Adhesive Film Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period.
