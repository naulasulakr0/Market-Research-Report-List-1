<p><p>The power lawn mowers market comprises three main types: gas-powered, battery-powered, and electric-powered mowers. Gas-powered mowers offer high power and range, suitable for larger lawns, but require more maintenance and produce emissions. Battery-powered mowers provide a quieter, eco-friendly option with the convenience of cordless operation, ideal for smaller to medium-sized yards. Electric-powered mowers are efficient for small to medium areas, offering consistent performance and lower maintenance, but are limited by cord length and distance from power sources.</p></p>
