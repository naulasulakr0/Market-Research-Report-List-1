<p><strong>The Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Mixed Mortar</li><li>Dry Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mortar markets are categorized into wet mixed mortar and dry mortar, each serving distinct applications. Wet mixed mortar is prepared on-site, combining cement, sand, and additives for immediate use in construction. It offers flexibility for specific job requirements but may have a shorter shelf life. In contrast, dry mortar is pre-bagged and composed of blended components, allowing for longer storage and consistent quality. This type is ideal for large-scale projects requiring efficient, uniform application, enhancing overall productivity in construction.</p></p>
