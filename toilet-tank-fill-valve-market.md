<p><strong>The Toilet Tank Fill Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Ball</li><li>Float Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toilet tank fill valve market primarily features two types: float ball and float cup mechanisms. The float ball type uses a large, buoyant ball attached to a lever to regulate water flow, ensuring the tank fills to the desired level before shutting off. The float cup design, on the other hand, employs a smaller float that moves up and down within a cylindrical assembly, offering a more compact and efficient operation. Both types are essential for effective toilet water management and efficiency.</p></p>
