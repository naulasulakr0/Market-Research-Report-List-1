<p><p>The turbo compressor market is primarily segmented into single-stroke and two-stroke categories. Single-stroke turbo compressors are designed to complete one cycle per revolution, offering efficiency and compactness, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate pressure ratios. In contrast, two-stroke turbo compressors employ two cycles per revolution, allowing for higher performance in gas compression. Both types cater to various industries, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation, driven by the need for enhanced energy efficiency and operational reliability.</p></p>
