<p><p>The optical fiber patch cord market is categorized into two main types: single-mode fiber optical patch cords and multimode fiber optical patch cords. Single-mode fibers utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, multimode fibers support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distances and higher bandwidth within data centers and local area networks. Both types are essential for various applications across telecommunications and data communications industries.</p></p>
