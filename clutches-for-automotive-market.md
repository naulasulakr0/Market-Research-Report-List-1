<p><p>Clutches in the automotive market play a crucial role in engaging and disengaging the engine from the drivetrain. In manual transmissions, drivers control the clutch pedal for gear shifting. Automatic transmissions (AT) automate this process, using hydraulic systems for smooth gear changes. Automated Manual Transmissions (AMT) combine elements of manual and automatic systems, allowing for semi-automatic operation. Continuously Variable Transmissions (CVT) provide seamless gear shifts by using a belt-and-pulley system, enhancing efficiency and performance while minimizing power loss.</p></p>
