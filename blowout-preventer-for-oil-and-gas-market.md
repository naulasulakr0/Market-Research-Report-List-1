<p><p>A Blowout Preventer (BOP) is essential in the oil and gas industry to prevent uncontrolled fluid releases during drilling operations. The market consists of two main types: Annular Blowout Preventers and Ram Blowout Preventers. Annular BOPs seal the wellbore using a rubber-like packing element that adapts to different pipe sizes, providing versatile sealing. Ram BOPs utilize solid blocks (or "rams") to clamp down on the wellbore, offering robust protection against blowouts. Both types enhance safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
