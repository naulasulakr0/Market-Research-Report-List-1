<p><p>The Infrared Milk Analyzer market consists of two primary types: Near-Infrared (NIR) and Mid-Infrared (MIR) analyzers. NIR analyzers utilize wavelengths from around 700 to 2500 nm, providing rapid assessment of milk composition, focusing on parameters like fat, protein, and lactose. In contrast, MIR analyzers operate in a longer wavelength range (approximately 2500 to 25,000 nm), offering detailed analysis with higher accuracy for specific components. Both types are essential for quality control and nutritional assessment in the dairy industry.</p></p>
