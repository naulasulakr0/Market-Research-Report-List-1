<p><p>The Semiconductor Backend Visual Inspection System market comprises mainly two types: Optical-Based and Infrared Type. Optical-Based systems utilize visible light to detect defects on semiconductor wafers and packages, offering high-resolution imaging for precise evaluations. In contrast, Infrared Type systems leverage thermal imaging to identify discrepancies, particularly in materials that may not be visible to the naked eye. Both technologies play crucial roles in ensuring quality control and reliability in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
