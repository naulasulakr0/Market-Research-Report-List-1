<p><p>Electronic Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) is crucial for ensuring the functionality and quality of semiconductor devices. The market is primarily divided into two types: wafer testing and package testing. Wafer testing involves assessing the chips while they are still in the wafer form, identifying defects early in the production process. Package testing, on the other hand, evaluates the final packaged semiconductor components for performance and reliability. Both types play a vital role in the semiconductor manufacturing lifecycle, ensuring high-quality outputs.</p></p>
