<p><ul><li>Soft Ground</li><li>Hard Rock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic directional drilling rig market is categorized mainly into two types based on ground conditions: soft ground and hard rock. Soft ground rigs are designed for drilling in less compacted soils, utilizing features like augers and mud systems to stabilize the bore. In contrast, hard rock rigs are engineered for tougher geological conditions, employing more robust drill bits and higher torque capabilities to penetrate dense materials. Each type is tailored to meet specific challenges in construction and resource extraction projects.</p></p>
