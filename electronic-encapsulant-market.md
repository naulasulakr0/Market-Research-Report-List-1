<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Multicomponent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic encapsulant market primarily features two types: one-component and multicomponent systems. One-component encapsulants are pre-mixed formulations that require no additional components for curing, offering ease of use and consistent performance. In contrast, multicomponent encapsulants consist of separate parts, typically a resin and a hardener, which must be mixed before application. This type often provides enhanced properties, such as higher thermal stability and tailored mechanical strength, catering to specific electronic applications and improving overall product reliability.</p></p>
