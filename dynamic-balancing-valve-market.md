<p><ul><li>Manual Balancing Valves</li><li>Automatic Balancing Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic balancing valve market includes two primary types: manual balancing valves and automatic balancing valves. Manual balancing valves require manual adjustment to regulate flow and pressure in a piping system, providing control but requiring ongoing maintenance and oversight. In contrast, automatic balancing valves self-adjust to maintain optimal flow and pressure without manual intervention, enhancing efficiency and reducing installation time. Both types play crucial roles in improving system performance, energy efficiency, and overall HVAC and fluid distribution effectiveness.</p></p>
