<p><ul><li>Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB)</li><li>Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB)</li><li>Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-voltage circuit breaker market comprises three main types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs), Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCBs), and Air Circuit Breakers (ACBs). MCBs are designed for low-current protection in residential and commercial applications, providing overcurrent and overload protection. MCCBs cater to moderate current capacities and are used in industrial settings, offering adjustable settings for different loads. ACBs handle high current capacities and are suitable for large installations, providing robust protection against overloads and short circuits.</p></p>
