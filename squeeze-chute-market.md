<p><p>Squeeze chutes are essential tools in livestock handling, designed to immobilize animals for safe medical checks or treatments. There are two main market types for these chutes: slanted and vertical. Slanted chutes feature a gentle incline that encourages the animal to enter willingly, reducing stress. Vertical chutes, on the other hand, have a straight up-and-down design, providing more control but may require more forceful handling. Both types cater to specific needs in livestock management and operational efficiency.</p></p>
