<p><ul><li>Active Patch buzzer</li><li>Passive Patch buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patch buzzer market is divided into two main types: active and passive patch buzzers. Active patch buzzers generate sound when a voltage is applied, featuring built-in oscillators and requiring minimal external components. They are often used in alarms and notifications. Conversely, passive patch buzzers rely on an external audio signal to produce sound, making them versatile for various applications. Both types are utilized widely in electronics, automotive systems, and consumer products, each serving distinct sound indication needs.</p></p>
