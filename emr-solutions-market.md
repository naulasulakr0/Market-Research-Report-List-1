<p><p>The EMR solutions market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based EMR solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from any device with internet access, which simplifies software updates and maintenance. On-premise EMR systems, on the other hand, are installed locally on a healthcare facility's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more upfront investment and ongoing IT support. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
