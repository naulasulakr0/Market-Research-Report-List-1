<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol testing device market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless devices. Contact devices require direct interaction, where individuals blow into a mouthpiece to measure blood alcohol content (BAC). These are often used by law enforcement and in workplace settings. Contactless devices, on the other hand, utilize advanced technology, such as infrared sensors or breath zone detection, to assess BAC without physical contact. This type is gaining popularity for its convenience and hygiene, especially in public and crowded environments.</p></p>
