<p><strong>The Sheet Waterproofing Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Modified Bitumen Membrane</li><li>Synthetic Polymer Sheet Waterproofing Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheet waterproofing membrane market encompasses two primary types: polymer modified bitumen membranes and synthetic polymer sheet membranes. Polymer modified bitumen membranes combine traditional bitumen with polymer additives to enhance flexibility, durability, and adhesion, making them suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, synthetic polymer sheet waterproofing membranes, made from materials like PVC and TPO, offer superior resistance to UV rays, chemicals, and extreme weather, making them ideal for commercial and industrial projects. Both types ensure effective moisture protection and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
