<p><p>The biopharmaceutical blister packaging market includes several types: PVC, lidding foils, cold form, and PVDC. PVC, known for its durability, is commonly used in packaging due to its cost-effectiveness and availability. Lidding foils provide a secure seal and are often used for moisture-sensitive products. Cold form packaging offers superior barrier properties, safeguarding sensitive compounds from environmental factors. PVDC is renowned for its impressive moisture and oxygen barrier, making it ideal for preserving the integrity of biopharmaceuticals. Each type ensures effective protection and extends shelf life.</p></p>
