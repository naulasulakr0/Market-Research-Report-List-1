<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>Spring-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inflatable accumulators come in two primary market types: gravity and spring-type. Gravity-type accumulators rely on the weight of the fluid in a container to generate pressure, suitable for applications needing consistent force without mechanical components. On the other hand, spring-type accumulators use mechanical springs to store and release energy, providing more controlled and adjustable pressure levels. Both types serve unique applications in fluid power systems, offering solutions for energy storage, shock absorption, and system stabilization in various industries.</p></p>
