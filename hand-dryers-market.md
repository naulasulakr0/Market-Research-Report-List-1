<p><p>The hand dryer market primarily consists of two types: warm air hand dryers and jet-air hand dryers. Warm air hand dryers utilize a gentle stream of heated air to evaporate moisture, providing a comfortable drying experience, but generally taking longer than other types. Jet-air hand dryers, on the other hand, release high-speed jets of air to quickly strip water off hands, promoting efficiency and reduced drying time. Both types are popular in commercial settings, focusing on hygiene and sustainability.</p></p>
