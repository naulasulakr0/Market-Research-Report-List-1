<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Asset Tracking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID Equipment</li><li>HF RFID Equipment</li><li>UHF RFID Equipment</li><li>MW RFID Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID asset tracking encompasses several market types based on frequency range: </p><p>- **LF RFID Equipment (Low Frequency)** operates at 125-134 kHz, ideal for close-range tracking and used in livestock management and access control.</p><p>- **HF RFID Equipment (High Frequency)** functions at 13.56 MHz, suitable for applications like library systems and ticketing.</p><p>- **UHF RFID Equipment (Ultra High Frequency)** ranges from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, enabling long-range tracking, commonly used in retail and logistics.</p><p>- **MW RFID Equipment (Microwave)** operates above 1 GHz, often applied in advanced tracking and automated identification scenarios.</p></p>
