<p><p>The Car Traffic Sign Recognition System market can be categorized into three types: Colour-Based, Shape-Based, and Feature-Based systems. Colour-Based systems identify signs by analyzing dominant colors, enhancing recognition in varied lighting. Shape-Based systems focus on the geometric contours of signs, ensuring accurate detection regardless of color variations. Feature-Based systems leverage machine learning to analyze intricate characteristics and patterns in signs, providing a robust detection mechanism suitable for complex environments. These distinctions reflect varying technological approaches in enhancing road safety.</p></p>
