<p><strong>The Medical Lithotripsy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intracorporeal Lithotripsy</li><li>Extracorporeal Lithotripsy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical lithotripsy device market is primarily segmented into two types: intracorporeal and extracorporeal lithotripsy. Intracorporeal lithotripsy involves the direct application of energy within the body to fragment stones, utilizing techniques like laser or ultrasound. In contrast, extracorporeal lithotripsy employs shock waves generated outside the body to target and break up stones without invasive procedures. Both methods are essential for treating urinary stones, with their effectiveness and patient recovery times influencing market dynamics and preferences among healthcare providers.</p></p>
