<p><p>The acrylic latex market comprises various types, including fluid acrylics and heavy-body acrylics. Heavy-body acrylics, on the other hand, offer a thicker consistency, ideal for impasto techniques and creating textured surfaces. Additionally, the market includes other specialized acrylic formulations, such as soft-body and acrylic gouache, catering to diverse artistic needs and preferences among painters and creators.</p></p>
