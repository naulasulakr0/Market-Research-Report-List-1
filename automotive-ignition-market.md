<p><p>The automotive ignition market encompasses various components, including ignition switches and ignition coils. Ignition switches are pivotal for starting the engine, allowing the flow of electrical current to the ignition system. Ignition coils, on the other hand, transform low-voltage battery power into high-voltage energy needed to ignite the air-fuel mixture in the engine's cylinders. Both components are essential for efficient engine performance and reliability, with ongoing advancements aimed at improving their functionality and durability in modern vehicles.</p></p>
