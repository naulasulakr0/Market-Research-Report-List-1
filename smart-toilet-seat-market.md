<p><p>The smart toilet seat market is divided into two main heating types: storage heating and instantaneous heating. Storage heating features a built-in reservoir that heats water in advance, providing a consistent temperature for each use, making it suitable for households with multiple users. In contrast, instantaneous heating heats water on demand, offering a more compact design and energy efficiency by eliminating continuous heating. Both types aim to enhance user comfort and hygiene in modern bathrooms.</p></p>
