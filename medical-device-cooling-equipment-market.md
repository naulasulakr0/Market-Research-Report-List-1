<p><p>The medical device cooling equipment market comprises two main types: liquid-based cooling systems and air-based cooling systems. Liquid-based cooling systems utilize fluids to absorb and transfer heat from medical devices, ensuring optimal temperature maintenance for sensitive equipment. In contrast, air-based cooling systems rely on air circulation to dissipate heat, often incorporating fans and heat exchangers. Both systems are essential for protecting electronic components in medical devices, enhancing performance, and prolonging operational life in various healthcare applications.</p></p>
