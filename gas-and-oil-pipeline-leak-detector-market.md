<p><p>The gas and oil pipeline leak detector market comprises several technologies. The Mass-Volume Balance Method detects leaks by comparing the input and output volumes of fluid, identifying discrepancies. Acoustic/Ultrasonic methods utilize sound wave alterations to pinpoint leaks, while Fiber Optics employ light transmission properties to sense changes indicative of leaks. Vapor Sensing detects gas emissions through specific vapor signatures, providing real-time monitoring. Each method offers unique advantages for enhanced safety and efficiency in pipeline management.</p></p>
