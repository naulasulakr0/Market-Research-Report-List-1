<p><p>NTC thermistors (Negative Temperature Coefficient thermistors) are crucial for temperature sensing and compensation in automotive applications. Radial type thermistors feature leads that extend vertically for easy PCB mounting. Diode type thermistors integrate thermistor functions with diodes for enhanced temperature detection. SMD (Surface Mount Device) type thermistors are designed for compact circuit boards, enabling space-efficient designs. Other types include encapsulated and surface-mounted thermistors, catering to diverse automotive needs, enhancing performance and reliability across various vehicle systems.</p></p>
