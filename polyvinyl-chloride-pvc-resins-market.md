<p><ul><li>Suspension</li><li>Emulsion</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC resins market comprises three main types: Suspension PVC (S-PVC), Emulsion PVC (E-PVC), and Blended PVC. S-PVC is produced through suspension polymerization and is widely used for rigid applications like pipes and fittings. E-PVC is created through emulsion polymerization, offering flexibility and is often used in coatings and adhesives. Blended PVC combines S-PVC and E-PVC to achieve a balance of properties for various applications, including cable insulation and consumer goods, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
