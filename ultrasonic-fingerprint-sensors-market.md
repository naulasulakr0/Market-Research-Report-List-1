<p><p>Ultrasonic fingerprint sensors come in two primary market types: under the glass and under plastic. Under the glass sensors are embedded within the glass surface of devices, providing an aesthetically appealing and seamless design while enabling high-precision fingerprint recognition even through protective coverings. In contrast, under plastic sensors are designed to operate effectively within plastic casings, often found in budget devices. Both types leverage ultrasonic technology to capture detailed fingerprint images, enhancing security and user convenience across various applications.</p></p>
