<p><p>The airport access control gates market encompasses two primary types: swing type and sliding type gates. Swing type gates operate through a pivot mechanism, offering wide openings and efficient access for passengers and staff. They are suited for areas requiring frequent access. Sliding type gates, on the other hand, operate by sliding horizontally, making them space-efficient and ideal for high-traffic zones. Both types enhance security while facilitating smooth passenger flow, contributing to effective airport operations and enhanced traveler experience.</p></p>
