<p><p>The titanium bone plates market is primarily categorized into two types: pure titanium and titanium alloys. Pure titanium plates are known for their excellent biocompatibility, corrosion resistance, and low weight, making them suitable for many orthopedic applications. In contrast, titanium alloy plates, which combine titanium with other metals like aluminum or vanadium, offer enhanced strength and elasticity, allowing for optimal support in load-bearing applications. Both types are crucial for effective bone fixation and healing in surgical procedures.</p></p>
