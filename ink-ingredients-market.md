<p><p>The ink ingredients market is categorized into four main types: solvent-based, water-based, oil-based, and UV-based inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for quick drying and vibrant colors, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and ideal for packaging and labels. Oil-based inks deliver rich colors and durability, often used in traditional printing methods. UV-based inks cure instantly under ultraviolet light, providing high durability and gloss, primarily utilized in specialty printing applications.</p></p>
