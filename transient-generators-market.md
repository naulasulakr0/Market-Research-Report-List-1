<p><p>Transient generators encompass various types of devices designed to produce short-duration electrical signals for testing and measurement. Surge generators create high-voltage transient pulses to simulate surge events, helping assess the robustness of electrical systems against voltage spikes. Burst generators, on the other hand, generate repeated short-duration signals to evaluate the response of electronic equipment under specific stress conditions. Both types play critical roles in ensuring the reliability and safety of modern electrical and electronic systems in various industries.</p></p>
