<p><p>The aluminium market comprises two primary types: pure aluminium and alloy aluminium. Pure aluminium, known for its excellent corrosion resistance and malleability, is widely used in applications requiring high electrical conductivity, like wiring. Alloy aluminium, which incorporates other metals to enhance properties such as strength and durability, is favored in construction, automotive, and aerospace sectors. Each market serves distinct needs, with pure aluminium focusing on specific physical properties and alloy aluminium offering improved performance for structural applications.</p></p>
