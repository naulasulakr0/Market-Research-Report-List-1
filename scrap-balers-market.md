<p><strong>The Scrap Balers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Balers</li><li>Horizontal Balers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scrap balers market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal balers. Vertical balers are compact and ideal for smaller operations, effectively compressing materials from the top down. They are energy-efficient and require minimal floor space. In contrast, horizontal balers are designed for high-capacity operations, capable of processing large volumes of material. They feed materials from the side, making them suitable for industrial settings. Both types enhance recycling efficiency by producing dense bales for easier handling and transportation.</p></p>
