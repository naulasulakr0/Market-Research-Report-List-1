<p><p>The Industrial Odor Control Systems market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Physical Odor Control Systems** utilize technologies such as activated carbon filters and biofilters to capture and eliminate odors without chemical reactions. </p><p>2. **Chemical Odor Control Systems** employ chemical agents to neutralize or mask odors, often through processes like oxidation or absorption. </p><p>3. **Biological Odor Control Systems** harness microorganisms to break down odor-causing compounds naturally, promoting an eco-friendly approach while effectively managing industrial odors.</p></p>
