<p><p>The Split-Body Ball Valves market includes two primary types: Flange Type and Butt Welding Type. Flange Type valves feature flanges for bolting to piping systems, allowing for easy installation and maintenance. They are commonly used in various industries due to their reliability and simplicity. Butt Welding Type valves, on the other hand, provide a seamless connection by welding directly to the pipe ends, ensuring minimal leakage and improved flow characteristics. Both types serve critical roles in fluid control applications across diverse sectors.</p></p>
